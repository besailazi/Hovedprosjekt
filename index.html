About

Boun Gelato is one of the oldest gelaterias in town. It was grounded in 1969 with the name "Beograd" that was later changed to "Buon Gelato". It's in the heart of the city of Novi Pazar.

It has old traditions with ice cream and has allways been providing the best one in town. This Ice Cafe has the biggest varity of ice cream flavors to choose from. In Boun Gelato every scoop is a celebration!

To make the ice cream they choose the best products the market offers and freshly prepare it with no preservatives.
